Exercise illustrations
======================

Source:  Everkinetic — https://github.com/everkinetic/data
License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0)
         https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

Every main lift and every swap alternative in the app is illustrated with two
frames (target muscle stretched, then contracted). The SVG files here are
redistributed unmodified; the app recolours them at display time (CSS invert +
screen blend) to read on a dark background. The files themselves are byte-for-
byte as published.

Under CC BY-SA 4.0 these illustrations, and any adaptation, stay under the same
licence. Attribution is shown in-app on each exercise's detail screen.
